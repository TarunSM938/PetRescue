Search Lost Pets Feature - Manual Testing Guide
==============================================

Contact Reporter Modal Testing
------------------------------

1. Test Modal Opening
   - Navigate to the Find Pets page
   - Click on any "Contact Reporter" button on a pet card
   - Verify that the modal opens correctly

2. Test Modal Content
   - Verify that the pet name is displayed correctly
   - Verify that the pet type and color are displayed
   - Verify that the reporter name is displayed
   - Verify that the privacy notice is visible

3. Test Contact Information
   - Verify that phone and email fields are displayed
   - Verify that the copy buttons are present for both phone and email
   - Test clicking on the phone number (should initiate a call on mobile)
   - Test clicking the copy buttons (should copy to clipboard)

4. Test Security & Privacy
   - Verify that contact information is only shown to authenticated users
   - Verify that unauthenticated users are prompted to sign in
   - Verify that the privacy notice is clearly visible

5. Test Alternative Contact Options
   - For authenticated users, verify that "View Full Report" and "Send Message" buttons are available
   - For unauthenticated users, verify that "Sign In to Contact" button is available

6. Test Modal Closing
   - Verify that the modal can be closed with the X button
   - Verify that the modal can be closed with the Close button
   - Verify that the modal can be closed by clicking outside the modal

7. Test Accessibility
   - Verify that all buttons are keyboard accessible
   - Verify that the modal can be navigated with keyboard
   - Verify that screen readers can access all content

8. Test Responsive Design
   - Verify that the modal displays correctly on mobile devices
   - Verify that the modal displays correctly on tablet devices
   - Verify that the modal displays correctly on desktop devices
