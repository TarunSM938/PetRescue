Our Mission

At PetRescue, we believe every animal deserves a loving home. Our mission is to rescue animals in need, provide them with medical care, and find them forever families.

Since our founding, we've helped over 10,000 animals find their forever homes. But we can't do it without your support. Your donations directly fund:

  • Medical care for rescued animals
  • Food and supplies for animals in our care
  • Spay and neuter programs to reduce pet overpopulation
  • Community education and outreach programs
  • Emergency rescue operations
