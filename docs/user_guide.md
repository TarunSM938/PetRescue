## Getting Started

### Registration
To use PetRescue, you'll need to create an account:

1. Click on the "Register" button in the top right corner of the homepage
2. Fill in the required information:
   - Username
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Password (must be at least 8 characters)
3. Click "Sign Up"
4. You'll be redirected to the login page where you can sign in with your new credentials

### Login
If you already have an account:

1. Click on the "Login" button in the top right corner
2. Enter your username/email and password
3. Click "Login"
4. You'll be redirected to the homepage

## Reporting a Lost Pet

If your pet is missing, you can create a report to help others find them:

1. After logging in, click on the "Report a Lost Pet" button on the homepage
2. Fill in the required information:
   - Pet type (dog, cat, bird, rabbit, or other)
   - Breed (if known)
   - Primary color
   - Location where the pet was last seen
   - Date when the pet was lost
   - Your phone number for contact
3. Add optional information:
   - Description of the pet (distinguishing features, behavior, etc.)
   - Photos of the pet (helpful for identification)
4. Review your information
5. Click "Report Lost Pet"

**Important Notes:**
- All fields marked with * are required
- Image files must be in JPG or PNG format
- Maximum file size per image is 5MB
- You can upload multiple photos

## Reporting a Found Pet

If you've found a lost pet, you can help reunite them with their family:

1. After logging in, click on the "Report a Found Pet" button on the homepage
2. Fill in the required information:
   - Pet type (dog, cat, bird, rabbit, or other)
   - Breed (if known)
   - Primary color
   - Location where the pet was found
   - Date when the pet was found
   - Your phone number for contact
3. Add optional information:
   - Description of the pet (condition, behavior, etc.)
   - Photos of the pet (helpful for identification)
4. Review your information
5. Click "Report Found Pet"

**Important Notes:**
- All fields marked with * are required
- Image files must be in JPG or PNG format
- Maximum file size per image is 5MB
- You can upload multiple photos

## Searching for Pets

You can search for lost or found pets in your area:

1. Click on the "Search for Pets" button on the homepage
2. Use the search filters to narrow down results:
   - Pet type
   - Breed
   - Color
   - Location
   - Date range
3. Click "Search"
4. Browse through the search results
5. Click on any pet to view detailed information
6. If you see a pet that might be yours or one you've found, you can contact the reporter using the phone number provided

**Search Tips:**
- Use specific locations for better results
- Try different color descriptions (e.g., "golden" instead of "yellow")
- Check regularly as new reports are added daily

## Managing Your Reports

You can view and manage all pet reports you've created:

1. After logging in, click on "My Reports" in the navigation menu
2. You'll see a list of all your pet reports
3. For pending reports (not yet reviewed by admins):
   - Click "Edit" to modify the report
   - Click "Delete" to remove the report
4. For accepted or rejected reports:
   - You can view the details but cannot edit or delete them

**Report Statuses:**
- **Pending**: Waiting for admin review
- **Accepted**: Verified and visible in search results
- **Rejected**: Not approved by admin (contact support if you believe this was an error)

## Editing Your Profile

You can update your personal information at any time:

1. After logging in, click on "My Profile" in the navigation menu
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update any information you'd like to change:
   - Bio (tell us about your experience with pets)
   - Location
   - Birth date
4. Click "Update Profile"

## Troubleshooting

### Image Upload Issues
**Problem**: Images won't upload
**Solutions**:
- Check that your image is in JPG or PNG format
- Ensure the file size is less than 5MB
- Try refreshing the page and uploading again
- Clear your browser cache and try again

### Login Problems
**Problem**: Can't log in with correct credentials
**Solutions**:
- Check that Caps Lock is off
- Ensure you're using the correct username/email
- Try resetting your password
- Clear your browser cache and cookies

### Report Not Showing in Search
**Problem**: Your report isn't appearing in search results
**Explanation**: 
- New reports must be reviewed and approved by admins before appearing in search
- This process typically takes 1-2 business days
- You'll receive a notification when your report is approved or rejected

### Contacting Support
If you're experiencing issues not covered in this guide:
1. Click on "Contact Us" in the footer
2. Fill out the contact form with details about your issue
3. Our support team will respond within 24-48 hours

## Best Practices

1. **Provide accurate information**: The more accurate details you provide, the better chance of reuniting pets with their families
2. **Upload clear photos**: Good quality photos significantly improve identification chances
3. **Update your reports**: If you find your pet or new information becomes available, update your report
4. **Be responsive**: Keep your phone available for calls from people who might have found your pet
5. **Check regularly**: Review search results regularly for pets that might be yours

## Privacy and Security

- Your personal information is protected and will only be used for pet rescue purposes
- Phone numbers are only visible to verified users who view your pet reports
- We never share your information with third parties without your consent
